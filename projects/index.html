  • Project Share What You Know

    This project is to create an online source for IIT KGP CS students. The main idea behind this project is to create a network where users login and post materials with a respective subject tag, and these materials will be rated by other users and based on the user ratings the material can replace the existing material for that topic(if the rating of this material is less than new material), else the posted material will be automatically deleted after few days. Thus helping students to have the best material available and save their time and space(If we have more than one material for a subject there is a chance that users may get confused which to read and it also occupies data on the database.) and if two or materials are good at differents parts of the same topic, then neccessary actions will be taken to merge the materials on suggestions.

    Subject Tag

    Subject tag is nothing but a means to specify which topic the given material discusses about. There is a chance to raise newer subject tags, which are later submitted for review and based on their ratings, they may become a permanent tag.
    And there will only be one material for a tag.User can submit a material for a tag without a material, but it has to cross a threshold rating4/10 to get into the database.
